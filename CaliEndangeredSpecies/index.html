California's Endangered Species Areas

These maps show the areas of three endangered species with the three bright colors. The thin blue lines represent the proclamation of protected lands in the state of California. I created a buffer around the endangered areas where they are in contact or border with very populated counties. The buffers are only present if these populated counties lie within 2 kilometers of the endangered areas. I also created an intersection to show where the endangered areas coincide with the protected areas. In order to do this I unioned all endangered areas together to allow me to compare them to the proclamation areas and proximity to populated counties. I used Census data for county populations and I selected those with +1 million population using a query. I then created a new layer containing only the populous counties.
